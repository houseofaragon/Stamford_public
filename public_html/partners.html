About Us

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford was found more than 40 years ago. Founded as a one man show by Dr. Les Krasnogor (the first board certified pulmonologist in the Stamford-area), Pulmonary Associates has grown to include six physicians, 2 nurse practioners, 1 physicians associate.

The physicians of Pulmonary Associates provided many innovations that now seem common place in pulmonary and critical care medicine. Pulmonary Associates physicians opened the first hospital blood gas laboratory in the region, developed an active bronchoscopy program, and established an innovative pulmonary rehabilitation program.

As the field of pulmonary medicine grew so did the practice of Pulmonary Associates of Stamford. All physicians at Pulmonary Associates are board certified in critical care medicine. Through our leadership in the Stamford Hospital Critical Care Unit, the Joint Commission awarded the Stamford Hospital ICU the prestigious Ernest A. Codman Award for quality and safety to patients. Partnering with Stamford Hospital, Pulmonary Associates has provided the leadership and clinical services for the CT Center for Sleep Medicine. Pulmonary Associates physicians also have expertise and practice in allergy/immunology and hospice and palliative care medicine.

For more than five decades, Pulmonary Associates of Stamford has recruited only the graduates of the finest academic pulmonary, critical, and sleep medicine fellowships. All our physicians are multiboarded and hold academic appointments at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons.

All the while, the physicians provided the highest patient centered care for an array of pulmonary conditions.

Our Partners

Stamford Hospital

Stamford Hospital is a 305-bed, not-for-profit hospital providing area residents (Fairfield and Westchester counties) with access to the latest technology using a compassionate, patient-centered care approach. The physicians of Pulmonary Associates of Stamford serve in a number of key roles at Stamford Hospital.
See more at: // http://www.stamfordhospital.org

Connecticut Center for Sleep Medicine

The Connecticut Center for Sleep Medicine at Stamford Hospital is accredited by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine. The physicians of Pulmonary Associates of Stamford provide the clinical and administrative services for the Connecticut Center for Sleep Medicine. This six-bed facility is located in its own suite on the third floor of Stamford Hospital. Our center began 18 years ago as a sleep apnea program, and has evolved into an advanced full-service sleep center with national accreditation.

Bennett Cancer Center—Stamford Hospital

The physicians of Pulmonary Associates of Stamford are often the first specialty physicians evaluating patients for lung cancer and pulmonary nodules. Integrated with Bennett Cancer Center team, Pulmonary Associates physicians offer an array of advanced diagnostic techniques such as endobronchial ultrasound, navigational bronchoscopy, ultrasound guided biopsies and thoracentesis. Pulmonary Associates is able to coordinate care with a dedicated Thoracic Oncology Nurse Navigator and are critical components of the Stamford Hospital Tumor Board.

Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeon

All Pulmonary Associates of Stamford Physicians hold academic appointments at the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons and are active in faculty for Columbia medical students and the internal medicine residency program at Stamford Hospital. We frequently have students and residents on rounds with us or in our office. Teaching young physicians helps us to stay current and be the best physicians we can be.

All physicians at Pulmonary Associates are board certified in critical care medicine and provide the highest patient centered care for an array of pulmonary conditions
