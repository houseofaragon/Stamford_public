John A. Kazianis, MD

Dr. John Kazianis grew up in Rhode Island and graduated with Honors from Brown University. He traveled to Nashville, TN and graduated from Vanderbilt University School of Medicine in 2006. Dr. Kazianis then moved to New York City where he completed a residency in internal medicine followed by a fellowship in pulmonary, critical care, and sleep medicine all at the Mount Sinai Medical Center. In 2012, Dr. Kazianis joined Pulmonary Associates of Stamford. He is very involved in medical student education. His clinical interests include critical care, lung cancer, pulmonary nodules, COPD, sarcoidosis, asthma during pregnancy, and sleep apnea.
