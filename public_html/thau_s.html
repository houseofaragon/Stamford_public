Steven A. Thau, MD

Dr. Steven A. Thau graduated from Yeshiva University and the Albert Einstein School of Medicine. He completed his post graduate training in internal medicine at the University of Pennsylvania and his fellowship in Pulmonary, Critical Care, and Sleep Medicine at Yale. Dr. Thau joined Pulmonary Associates of Stamford in 2001 and is board certified in Internal Medicine, Pulmonary Diseases, Critical Care Medicine and Sleep Medicine. Dr. Thau serves the Medical Director of the Intermediate Care Unit and the Associate Medical Director of Sleep Medicine. He also serves on the Stamford Hospital Ethics Committee and additionally board certified in hospice and palliative care medicine. Dr. Thau has received numerous awards while at Stamford Hospital including Rookie of Year, Teacher of the Year, and Attending of the Year. Dr. Thau’s clinical interests include critical care, asthma, pulmonary hypertension, pulmonary fibrosis, sleep apnea, restless leg syndrome, electronic medical records, and medical ethics.
