Patient Information

Insurance Information

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford treats a wide range of pulmonary (lung) diseases including cough, shortness of breath, asthma, COPD, lung cancer, pulmonary nodules (abnormal X-rays and CT Scans), pulmonary fibrosis, pulmonary hypertension, pleural effusions and sarcoidosis. Our new patients have a 1 hour consultation with one of our physicians who will be in close communication with your primary doctor or referring physician.

Directions

In coordination with the CT Center for Sleep Medicine, Pulmonary Associates of Stamford treats and diagnosis a wide range of sleep conditions including sleep apnea, snoring, insomnia, restless leg syndrome, and narcolepsy. We treat both children and adults. We have a comprehensive CPAP management team and are delighted to see patients as new consultations or to continue care for patients who have already been diagnosed with a sleep disorder. All studies are interpreted by a physician board certified in sleep medicine

What to bring?

Our board certified allergy team provides a full array of allergy services including allergy consultations, skin testing, immunotherapy, and infusion services. Our allergy team treats both adolescents and adults and has expertise in drug allergies and complex allergy patients.

All physicians at Pulmonary Associates are board certified in critical care medicine and provide the highest patient centered care for an array of pulmonary conditions
