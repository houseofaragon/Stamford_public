Patient Information

Insurance Information

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford accepts nearly all major insurance plans including Medicare. For questions regarding coverage please contact our office 203-348-2437 or your insurance provider. Co-payments and deductibles are expected at the time of your appointment.

Directions

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford

Our offices are located on the first floor of Stamford Hospital at the entrance of the Bennett Cancer Center. We are fully wheelchair accessible. Please enter the hospital at the Admitting Entrance by the Cafeteria and follow signs to Pulmonary Associates.

CT Center for Sleep Medicine

Our office is located on the third floor of Stamford Hospital. Please enter the hospital at the Admitting Entrance by the Cafeteria and proceed to elevator C. Take elevator to the 3rd; we are the first office on the right. We are fully wheelchair accessible.

What to bring?

Please try to arrive 15 minutes prior to your appointment to complete any necessary forms. Please bring a copy of your insurance card, driverâ€™s license, and an updated list of your medications. For your first appointment, please bring any records including laboratory reports, previous sleep studies and pulmonary function tests, as well as radiology reports. For X-rays and CT Scans not performed at Stamford Hospital, Tully Health Center, or Darien Imaging, please try to bring a CD copy of the tests so we can personally review them. For Sleep Medicine patients, we encourage you to bring your CPAP machine and equipment as well as your machine download-chip.

For new patients, we encourage you to use or patient portal to complete forms prior to your visit. Please ask for login information at the time of your appointment or call our office for more information.
