Our Services

Outpatient

Pulmonary Consultations

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford treats a wide range of pulmonary (lung) diseases including cough, shortness of breath, asthma, COPD, lung cancer, pulmonary nodules (abnormal X-rays and CT Scans), pulmonary fibrosis, pulmonary hypertension, pleural effusions and sarcoidosis. Our new patients have a one hour consultation with one of our physicians who will be in close communication with your primary doctor or referring physician.

Sleep Medicine Consultations

In coordination with the CT Center for Sleep Medicine (203-276-2300), Pulmonary Associates of Stamford treats and diagnosis a wide range of sleep conditions including sleep apnea, snoring, insomnia, restless leg syndrome, and narcolepsy. We have experience treating both children and adults. Our Sleep Center team includes five physicians, one nurse practitioner with years of sleep medicine experience, and one nurse/ respiratory therapist. We are delighted to see patients as consultations or to continue to care for patients who have already been diagnosed with a sleep disorder. The CT Center for Sleep Medicine offers an array of testing including home sleep studies, in-lab sleep studies, actigrophy, and multiple sleep latency testing. All studies are interpreted by a physician board certified in sleep medicine

Allergy Consultations

Our board certified allergy team provides a full array of allergy services including allergy consultations, skin testing, immunotherapy, and infusion services. Our allergy team treats both adolescents and adults and has expertise in drug allergies and complex allergy patients.

Pulmonary Function Testing

We provided in-office spirometry and basic pulmonary stress testing. Together with Stamford Hospital, we coordinate testing at both the main hospital and Tully Health Center. We provide the latest in pulmonary function testing including spirometry, lung volumes, diffusion testing, six minute walk testing, cardiopulmonary exercise stress testing, and exhaled NO testing. We also have the latest in bronchodilation testing including methacholine challenge testing and mannitol challenge testing.

Bronchoscopy and Pulmonary Procedures

The physicians of Pulmonary Associates of Stamford are able to perform the latest and most innovative bronchoscopy and pulmonary procedures. We have extensive experience in advanced bronchoscopy techniques including endobronchial ultrasound (linear and radial probes), navigation bronchoscopy, endobronchial valves, pleural biopsies, PleurX catheters and thoracentesis.

Clinical Trials Program

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford has a robust and long history of performing clinical trials. We have participated in trials on COPD, asthma, pulmonary fibrosis, and lung infections. Even for patients not enrolled in trials, our clinical trials participation helps to keep all our physicians on the cutting edge of medicine. For more information, please call Liz Sachs, NP 203 348 2437 or email pulmonarystamford@gmail.com.

Inpatient

Critical Care Consultations

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford physicians lead a mutidiscinplinary team of providers for patients in the Stamford Hospital Critical Care Unit. Pulmonary Associates of Stamford physicians are present 365 days per year and direct the care of patients in the Critical Care Unit. Our physicians are all board certified in critical care and experts in caring for the sickest patients in the hospital. We have expertise in managing patients with respiratory failure, septic shock, complex infections and hemodynamic instability. Our physicians are trained advanced critical care procedures and how to use the latest intensive care unit technologies.

Pulmonary Consultations

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford operates the Pulmonary Consultation service at Stamford Hospital. We treat a range diseases from chronic obstructive pulmonary disease and lung cancer to interstitial lung disease, sarcoidosis, community acquired pneumonias, and the whole host of opportunistic infections in compromised hosts. We are available to see new patients as well as care for our patient when they are hospitalized.

PROCEDURE SERVICES

Pulmonary Associates of Stamford physicians are often called upon to perform a range of procedures for patients admitted to Stamford Hospital. We utilize the latest technologies in pulmonary diagnostics including advanced bronchoscopy (endobronchial ultrasound and navigational bronchoscopy) and pleural procedures including thoracentesis, chest tubes, pleual biopsies, and PleurX catheters. Our critical care physicians are skilled in the placement of central venous catheters, arterial catheters, temporary dialysis catheters, and intubation.

All physicians at Pulmonary Associates are board certified in critical care medicine and provide the highest patient centered care for an array of pulmonary conditions
